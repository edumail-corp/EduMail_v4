# University Pitch Runbook

## Main Story

EduMailAI helps university teams handle inbound student email faster while keeping humans in control.

The message to land:
- staff see the full queue in one place
- AI helps prepare grounded replies instead of guessing
- policy and calendar documents stay attached to the work
- final approval remains with a human team member

## Demo Flow

1. `/`
Show the product name and explain the problem in plain language:
"Student services teams lose time switching between inboxes, policy PDFs, and internal notes."

2. `/sign-in`
Move through sign-in quickly.
Use the `Operations Admin` demo account first if the built-in staff access options are visible.

3. `/dashboard`
Frame this as the control center:
- queue pressure
- ownership
- knowledge-base health
- recent activity

4. `/dashboard/inbox`
Open the first featured case immediately:
- `Can prior study in English waive IELTS for Fall 2027 admissions?`

5. Featured case talking points
- show the original student message
- show the suggested reply
- call out the source citation and why it matters
- mention the staff note
- show regenerate reply as the "improve with oversight" moment
- explain that approval stays human

6. `/dashboard/knowledge-base`
Show that the reply is grounded in institutional guidance:
- `International Admissions Guidance 2026.pdf`
- one or two other policy documents

7. Close on one of:
- `/dashboard/activity` for traceability and operational visibility
- `/dashboard/admin` for staff access and governance

## What To Avoid Showing

- Settings unless asked directly
- Compose unless you need the backup story
- Redirect-only pages like Drafts or Escalations
- Advanced storage details in Admin unless someone asks
- Any live sync or live send step unless it has been verified that same day

## Rehearsal Checklist

- Open the app in English
- Be signed in before the meeting if needed
- Confirm the first inbox case is the admissions case
- Confirm dashboard metrics are populated
- Confirm recent activity is populated
- Confirm the knowledge-base page highlights matching documents from the cited case
- Confirm Admin opens with the operations admin account
- Keep a backup close:
  if Inbox misbehaves, switch to Dashboard -> Knowledge Base -> Activity
